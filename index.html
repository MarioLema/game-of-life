TOROIDAL GAME OF LIFE

The game of life is a simple game where cells are either dead or alive. In this version cells "age" the more generations they spend being alive or dead. The board has been designed to be toroidal, meaning the last column and the last row connect with their respective first row or column.

The game has several options like a binary black and white for alive and dead, a heatmap or a fun emoji version. See the legend below for more info and refer to Wikipedia for more info
